Message For You!

About The Website

Welcome to Message For You! Here, we believe in
the magic of heartfelt connections and the joy that
comes from sharing a thoughtful message with those
you care about. Whether it’s for a birthday,
Christmas, Valentine, or just to remind someone that
they are loved, we know that a simple, genuine card
can make all the difference. At Message For You, our
mission is to help you express what’s in your heart
in a way that’s meaningful and memorable because a meaningful message has the power to brighten anyone’s day.

Jhaycel Cammayo and Cherry Jane Boncales, third-year BSIT students at Arellano University Jose Abad Santos Camous, were the team behind developing this website. The website was developed specifically to fulfill a requirement for the subject course "Elective 3: Human Computer Interaction 2." This website started out in November. 4, 2024, and it was last updated on November. 11, 2024.
